Privacy Policy

Any changes made to this policy will become effective immediately upon being posted on this page. It is your responsibility to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how your information is protected. Your continued use of the website or services after any updates to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

Swedro Trading Institute collects information in order to provide and manage the services and products you request, including training programs, mentorship services, and educational resources related to financial markets. We use the information collected to communicate with you regarding your account, provide customer support, process your enrollment in our programs, and deliver relevant information related to our services. The information we gather also helps us understand your needs and preferences so that we can improve our website, educational programs, and overall user experience. Your information may also be used to send important service announcements, updates, or information about new programs and offerings that may be relevant to your interests.

The personal information we collect generally comes directly from you when you interact with our website or services. This information may include your name, contact details such as email address and phone number, and other information you provide while filling out forms, registering for programs, or contacting us for support. In some cases, we may also collect physical address information or other details necessary for administrative purposes related to our services. We may also collect information through cookies or similar technologies when you visit our website. Cookies are small data files stored in your browser that help us improve website functionality, personalize your experience, and analyze website usage patterns.

When you visit our website, certain technical information may be automatically recorded by our servers. This information may include your Internet Protocol (IP) address, browser type, pages visited, search queries, and the duration of your visit. This type of information helps us understand how users interact with our website and allows us to improve its performance and functionality. The information collected through cookies or log data does not typically identify you personally, but it helps us analyze website usage trends and enhance the overall user experience.

Swedro Trading Institute values your privacy and does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. However, your information may be shared with trusted service providers, contractors, or partners who assist us in operating our website or delivering our services. These third parties are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information and use it only for the purposes for which it was provided. In certain situations, we may also disclose your information if required by law, regulatory authorities, or legal processes, or if such disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, enforce our policies, or prevent misuse of our services.

You should be aware that if you voluntarily disclose personal information in public areas of the website, such as discussion forums, comments, or social media channels associated with our services, that information may be visible to other users and third parties. Swedro Trading Institute does not control how third parties may use information shared in public areas. Therefore, you should exercise caution when sharing personal details in such environments. You are also responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials and any login information associated with your use of our services.

Swedro Trading Institute takes reasonable measures to protect the security of the information you provide to us. We implement security procedures designed to safeguard personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. However, it is important to understand that no method of data transmission over the internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure. While we strive to protect your personal information using industry-standard practices, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of data transmitted through our website.

The inclusion of a link to another website does not imply endorsement, authorization, or affiliation with that site.

Swedro Trading Institute may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal obligations.
